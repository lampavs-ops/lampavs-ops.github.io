**Last updated: June 19, 2026**

Welcome to K-Unpacked. We care about your privacy.
This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect your name, email address,
or any other personal information directly.

However, third-party services we use may collect
some non-personal data automatically (like visit counts).

---

## 2. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand
how visitors use our site.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect:
- Pages you visit
- How long you stay
- Where you came from
- Your device and browser type
- Your general location (country or city level)

This data is anonymous. It cannot identify you personally.

Learn more: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Opt out: [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

---

## 3. Google AdSense (Advertising)

We may show ads through Google AdSense.
Google uses cookies to show ads that match your interests.

Learn more: [Google Ads Privacy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

Opt out: [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

---

## 4. Cookies

We do not create cookies ourselves.
Third-party services like Google Analytics and AdSense
may use cookies when you visit our site.

You can turn off cookies in your browser settings.
Some features of the site may not work if you do.

---

## 5. External Links

Our posts may include links to YouTube, Netflix,
and other websites.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices
of those external sites.
Please check their own privacy policies.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information
from children under 13.

If you believe a child has shared personal data with us,
please contact us so we can remove it.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to check this page occasionally.
