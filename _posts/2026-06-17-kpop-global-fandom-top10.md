## 🌍 Why Are K-Pop Fan Groups So Strong?

K-Pop fans do not just listen to music. They build **communities**. Here is why it works:

1. **Fan events** — Groups hold fan meetings, online concerts, and pop-up stores in cities around the world.
2. **Fan names and identity** — Being an "ARMY" or a "BLINK" feels like joining a special club.
3. **Social media** — Twitter (X), TikTok, Weverse, and Bubble apps keep fans close to their favorite artists every day.
4. **Streaming together** — Fans plan streaming parties to help their favorite songs get to the top of the charts.
5. **Photo cards in albums** — Fans love collecting photo cards. This makes people buy more albums!
