## ✍️ Writer — Park Ji-eun (박지은)

> 🎬 [**▶ Watch Queen of Tears behind-the-scenes on YouTube**](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Queen+of+Tears+behind+the+scenes+making+Park+Ji+eun)

### The Only Writer to Hold tvN's #1 Record Twice

Park Ji-eun has a record that no other Korean drama writer holds.

**Crash Landing on You** (2019–2020) — tvN all-time rating record at the time: 21.7%

**Queen of Tears** (2024) — tvN all-time rating record broken again: 24.85%

The same writer set tvN's highest rating record two times. No one else has done this.

Both shows share something. A love story in an impossible situation. Two people from different worlds. Walls that seem impossible to break. And two people who refuse to give up.

### How She Writes Love

There is a rule in every Park Ji-eun drama.

"Love must not come easily. The higher the obstacle, the more the audience wants them to make it."

In Queen of Tears, the obstacles are a terminal illness, memory loss, and a family conspiracy decades in the making. The harder the road, the sweeter the arrival.

## 🎬 The Directors — Kim Hee-won & Jang Young-woo

**Director Kim Hee-won** made *Vincenzo* (2021) — a crime comedy thriller. He then made Queen of Tears — a romantic melodrama. Two completely different genres, both executed at the highest level. He is one of the few Korean directors who can shift tone that completely and make it work.

**Director Jang Young-woo** is known for the *Sweet Home* series — horror, monsters, dark fantasy. That same eye for atmosphere and emotional tension made Queen of Tears feel grounded even when the plot went into dramatic territory.

Together, the two directors handled romance, thriller, and comedy — often in the same episode — without losing the emotional center.

| Director | Known for |
|----------|-----------|
| Kim Hee-won | Vincenzo (2021) · Queen of Tears (2024) |
| Jang Young-woo | Sweet Home series · Queen of Tears (2024) |

### Who Is He?

Kim Soo-hyun is one of the biggest names in Korean drama — and has been for over a decade.

Queen of Tears was his big comeback after completing his military service (2017–2019) and a quieter period of projects. Even before the show aired, his per-episode fee made headlines — widely reported as the highest in Korean drama history at the time.

The role of Hyun-woo is technically demanding. He starts as a man ready to walk away. He becomes a man who will not leave no matter what. And in between, he has to hide who he is from someone who has forgotten him. All of that lives in Kim Soo-hyun's face — without a speech, without explanation.

### Kim Ji-won (김지원) — Hong Hae-in

> 📸 **Official SNS:** [Instagram @k_jiwon9](https://www.instagram.com/k_jiwon9)

> 🎬 [**▶ Watch Kim Ji-won's best Queen of Tears moments on YouTube**](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Kim+Ji+won+Queen+of+Tears+best+scenes)

### Who Is She?

Kim Ji-won is a patient actress.

She debuted in 2011. For over a decade, she was consistently praised in every show she appeared in. But she was always supporting the lead, not being one. Descendants of the Sun. My Liberation Notes. Arthdal Chronicles. She shone in every role — but the center was always someone else's.

Queen of Tears gave her the center. She took it completely.

Hae-in is a character who communicates through silence. She does not have long speeches. She has micro-expressions — a slight shift in the eyes, a jaw that barely tightens. Kim Ji-won delivered all of it. The result: Baeksang Arts Award for Best Actress 2024.

### Kwak Dong-yeon (곽동연) — Hong Soo-cheol

> 📸 **Official SNS:** [Instagram @dy.__.yeon](https://www.instagram.com/dy.__.yeon)

### Who Is He?

The biggest surprise of the whole show.

Hong Soo-cheol is Hae-in's younger brother. He tries very hard to be powerful and important. He fails, constantly, in the most entertaining ways possible. Every scene he is in becomes funnier than it should be.

Kwak Dong-yeon was already a respected actor from Vincenzo and Big Mouth. But Queen of Tears turned him into a fan favorite. The rule became simple: "When Soo-cheol appears, something funny is about to happen."

### Who Is He?

Yoon Eun-sung is the show's main source of tension. A former Wall Street analyst. Obsessively connected to Hae-in's past. He returns to Korea with a plan.

Park Sung-hoon made the entire country angry when he played a bully in The Glory. He brings the same intensity to Yoon Eun-sung — but this character is more complicated. Is he a villain? A victim? The show keeps you guessing.

## 🎭 Why the "Two Kims" Chemistry Worked

The biggest reason Queen of Tears succeeded is the chemistry between Kim Soo-hyun and Kim Ji-won.

The two had not appeared in a drama together since My Love from the Star — in 2013. Fans waited over 10 years for this pairing.

The moment they looked at each other on screen, audiences felt it. Something clicked that cannot be scripted.

Chemistry like this does not come from the writing. It comes from two actors who trust each other completely. That trust is visible in every shared scene.
