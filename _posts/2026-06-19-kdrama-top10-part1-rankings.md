## 🏆 How Big Is "Big"?

Numbers this large are hard to picture. Let's put them in context.

| Squid Game S1 Hours | What That Means |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1,650,450,000 hrs | Over **182,000 years** of total watch time |
| 1,650,450,000 hrs | **2.6×** more than Netflix's next biggest show at the time |
| 1,650,450,000 hrs | #1 in **94 countries** at the same time |

And Queen of Tears? In 2024, it was the **2nd most-watched show on all of Netflix** — English or non-English — beaten only by Bridgerton Season 3.

## 🌍 Why Does the World Love K-Dramas?

- **Short and complete** — Most are 16 episodes. No filler. No years of waiting.
- **High production value** — Cinematography, OSTs, and costumes that rival Hollywood.
- **Real emotions** — K-dramas are not afraid to make you cry.
- **Strong characters** — Women and underdogs drive these stories.
- **Netflix's global reach** — Subtitles in 30+ languages means anyone can watch.
