## 🌸 장 (Jang) — Jang Wonyoung · IVE

![Jang Wonyoung IVE](/assets/img/posts/jang-wonyoung-profile.jpg)
*Jang Wonyoung — IVE (Starship Entertainment)*

> 🎬 [**▶ Watch 'IVE ELEVEN' Official MV on YouTube**](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=IVE+ELEVEN+Official+MV)

### Who is she?

Jang Wonyoung is everywhere in Korea right now. She is on TV, on billboards, and in conversations about K-pop beauty standards. But she is not new.

She first appeared on the survival show **Produce 48** in 2018 — and **finished #1**. She was only **13 years old**. Then she debuted with IZ*ONE. After that group ended, she joined IVE in 2021.

By 2026, she is one of the most advertised celebrities in all of South Korea. Her nickname? **"Lucky Vicky."** She calls herself lucky, and many people agree.

### ✨ What makes her special?

She co-wrote the lead single "XOXZ" from IVE's fourth EP *Ive Secret*, which Billboard later named one of the best K-pop songs of 2025.

She was the most exposed advertisement model in South Korea in 2025, working with brands like Dior, Dyson Korea, Bulgari, and Medicube, among others.

She also has the rare ability to make brands succeed just by being associated with them. After becoming an ambassador for Hapa Kristin in 2022, the brand saw a 40.3% sales increase — and the product became known as "the Jang Wonyoung lens."

![Karina aespa](/assets/img/posts/karina-aespa-profile.jpg)

### Who is she?

Karina is the leader of aespa — and many fans call her the "AI Goddess." That is because aespa's whole concept is about digital avatars living in a virtual world. Karina's look fits that concept perfectly. She looks almost unreal.

But off stage, she is known to be **quiet, a bit shy, and very funny** with her group members. She plays video games (League of Legends is her favorite), writes in a diary every day, and has a black belt in **taekwondo**.

In July 2025, Nike named her their global brand spokesperson — making her the first Korean celebrity and first female K-pop idol to hold that position.

Sullyoon is the youngest of the four — and arguably the biggest visual surprise story of the 4th generation.

She joined JYP Entertainment in 2020 after one of the **shortest trainee periods** in NMIXX. Before that, she had passed auditions at SM, YG, and multiple other companies. They all wanted her.

The moment she was revealed as a NMIXX member, K-pop Twitter lost it. People compared her to classical paintings. Korean netizens called her the "Doll from Daejeon."

## 💙 유 (Yu) — Yuna · ITZY

![Yuna ITZY](/assets/img/posts/yuna-itzy-profile.jpg)
*Yuna — ITZY (JYP Entertainment)*

> 🎬 [**▶ Watch 'ITZY DALLA DALLA' Official MV on YouTube**](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=ITZY+DALLA+DALLA+Official+MV)

### Who is she?

Yuna is ITZY's **maknae** — the youngest member. She is also the tallest in the group.

She was scouted at a music festival in 2016 and trained at JYP for three years. She debuted in 2019 at just 15 years old. Even before debut, she appeared in **BTS's Highlight Reel** alongside Jungkook.

Her personality is bright, loud, and full of energy. Fans love her for being unapologetically confident. She does not try to tone it down.

In March 2026, Yuna made her solo debut with the mini album *Ice Cream* — the first ITZY member to go solo.

## 🌟 Why Do These Four Get Grouped Together?

It is not random. These four got grouped together because:

1. **They are all active at the same time** — all debuted between 2019 and 2022.
2. **They are all considered top visuals** — Korean beauty standards, editorial looks, and brand value.
3. **They all do solo activities** — fashion, ads, MC work, solo music.
4. **They appeared together** — Wonyoung, Karina, Sullyoon, and Yuna have all shared stages at Korean year-end festivals.
5. **Korean fans made the nickname viral** — and it spread to international fans fast.
